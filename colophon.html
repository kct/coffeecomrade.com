Colophon

Coffee Comrade is written by Bryan Matthew Warren.

Philosophy

I write this weblog for several reasons, foremost of which to improve my communication skills and clarify my thoughts on coffee. I love coffee and the culture that surrounds it, but have an admittedly small working knowledge of the vast amount of intricacies.

I think Jeff Atwood skillfully summarized this notion in his article about the why's of writing.

Importantly, I hope to inspire in readers a love of– and participation in– coffee culture without the common snobbery and heavy-handedness.

Technical

I don't use any server-side weblog software; I have no use for 90% of their features. Instead, I write articles in plain text, then everything is gussied-up into a static site via Jekyll with some help from the jekyll_ext wrapper and several extensions.

For the truly curious, you can poke around in my source code on GitHub.

I encourage you to contact me with any feedback or questions. I don't offer a commenting system by choice, and Paul Ford's observations on the "Why Wasn't I Consulted?" problem should explain that decision.
